MILITARY GRADE SAFETY

How We Secure Your Shared Network

Security is our absolute priority. We insulate hosts' private networks from guest connections to ensure total safety and compliance.

Client Sandbox Isolation: Guests have absolutely zero visibility or contact with other local IP devices on your router.

Dynamic Speed Throttling: Allocates a smooth segment of your bandwidth so your home devices remain incredibly fast.

VPN Sandboxing

App implements a secure local Android VPN layer to filter and control outbound traffic metrics, preventing guests from tampering with home systems.

Auto-Disconnect

Once your paid time limit triggers, the local access portal instantly revokes connections automatically, protecting your margins.

Encrypted Handshakes

Payment references, wallet updates, and routing authentication flows are fully encrypted, securing transaction records.

Zero Log Policy

We value strict privacy constraints. No logging or inspection of client content traffic metrics occurs at any point.

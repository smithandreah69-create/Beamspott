BeamSpot Terms of Service

Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 14, 2026

1. Overview

BeamSpot ("we," "us," "our") operates a mobile application and website that connect people who want to share their internet connection ("Hosts") with people who want to pay for temporary internet access ("Guests"). This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and what choices you have, for both Hosts and Guests, whether or not you have created an account or installed the app.

2. Information We Collect

From Hosts

  • Google account information (name, email address, profile picture) when you sign in with Google
  • Payout details you provide, such as M-Pesa number, Airtel Money number, or bank account information
  • Network information needed to operate your shared connection, including WiFi SSID, BSSID (router hardware address), and connection type
  • Session and earnings data: how many guests connect, how long they stay connected, and amounts earned
  • Approximate signal, speed, and connection statistics measured on your own device while hosting

From Guests

  • Phone number provided for M-Pesa payment
  • Device identifier and local IP address, used only to authorize your specific device for the internet session you paid for
  • Session details: duration purchased, amount paid, and connection status

Location Data

The BeamSpot app requests location permission because Android requires this permission to scan for nearby WiFi networks — this is an operating system requirement, not a choice we make. We do not collect, store, or use your precise GPS location for any other purpose, and we do not track your location history.

3. How We Use Information

  • To create and manage your account
  • To operate the core function of the app: connecting Hosts and Guests, authorizing paid sessions, and enforcing session time limits
  • To process payments and payouts through our payment partner
  • To prevent a single network from being claimed by more than one Host at a time
  • To display real usage statistics (speed, guest count, earnings) to Hosts about their own shared connection
  • To investigate abuse, fraud, or violations of our Terms of Service

4. What We Do Not Do

  • We do not inspect, log, or store the content of internet traffic that passes through a Host's shared connection
  • We do not sell personal information to third parties
  • We do not use your data for targeted advertising

5. Third-Party Services

We rely on the following third parties to operate BeamSpot:

  • Google Sign-In — for Host account authentication
  • IntaSend — for processing M-Pesa and other payments
  • Railway — for hosting our backend server and database

Each of these providers processes certain data on our behalf under their own privacy and security practices.

6. Data Retention

We retain account and session data for as long as your account is active, and for a reasonable period afterward to comply with financial recordkeeping obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements. You may request deletion of your account and associated personal data at any time, subject to any legal or financial recordkeeping requirements that require us to retain certain records.

7. Your Choices

  • You can revoke location permission at any time in your device settings, though this will prevent the app's WiFi scanning features from working
  • You can request a copy of, correction to, or deletion of your personal data by contacting us
  • Hosts can stop sharing their connection and remove their listing at any time

8. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information, including encrypted connections between the app and our backend. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Children's Privacy

BeamSpot is not directed at children under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will update the "Last updated" date above when we do. Continued use of BeamSpot after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or wish to exercise any of the choices described above, contact us at the support channel provided within the BeamSpot app.
